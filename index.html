Linkalls

Simple social media scheduling and publishing platform for Facebook and Instagram.

What We Do

Linkalls is a SaaS platform that helps businesses and agencies plan, schedule and publish their social media content for Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts from a single dashboard.

Key Features

  • Schedule posts for Facebook Pages and Instagram accounts in advance.
  • Manage multiple pages and accounts from one dashboard.
  • Calendar view to see all upcoming posts at a glance.
  • Basic analytics for post performance (planned feature).

How It Works

  1. Clients securely connect their Facebook and Instagram business accounts using Meta Login.
  2. Create and schedule posts with text, images or videos for future dates and times.
  3. At the scheduled time, our platform publishes posts on behalf of the client using Meta APIs.
  4. Clients can review basic performance of their content inside the dashboard (planned feature).

Who Is It For?

  • Small and medium businesses that want to plan their social media content in advance.
  • Marketing agencies managing multiple client pages and accounts.
  • Freelancers handling several Facebook Pages and Instagram profiles.

About Akash Shakya

Linkalls is being developed by Akash Shakya as part of Akash Shakya Digital Services, a technology business based in Mainpuri, Uttar Pradesh, India. The goal of the platform is to make social media content planning and management easier for businesses and agencies.

How We Use Meta Platform Data

Our platform only uses Meta Platform Data for Facebook and Instagram accounts that clients explicitly connect to Linkalls. We use this data only to:

  • Create, schedule and publish posts on behalf of our clients to their connected pages and accounts.
  • Show basic performance metrics of posts inside the client dashboard (planned feature).

We do not sell, rent or share Meta Platform Data with third parties, and we do not use it for our own marketing or profiling. Clients can disconnect their accounts at any time, after which we revoke our API access.
